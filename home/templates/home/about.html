Our Mission

We strive to raise public awareness of dementia in order to improve the lives of people with memory loss.

Our Values

We value healthfulness and safety of our population. Regardless of personal circumstance, we believe each and every human has a right to a healthy life.

Our Team

We are a group of rising seniors at Huron High School in Ann Arbor, Michigan, dedicated to supporting those who suffer from mental illnesses such as dementia and Alzheimer’s disease through creativity and problem solving.

Our Program

Our program is a collection of puzzles and other activies which are first and foremost fun to solve, while improving cognitive ability. Our research has shown that social interaction is critical, and our programs aim to emphasize collaboration.
