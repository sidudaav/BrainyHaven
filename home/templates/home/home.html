How To Use Brainy Haven

Home

The “Home” page showcases important data surrounding dementia, our values, and beliefs. Here you can find a general overview of Brainy Haven's current operations.

About Us

The “About Us” page is where you can get more information on how we began and our goals for this project. You can also read more on us, the creators of Brainy Haven.

The Science

In the “The Science” page, you can read information about the brain and dementia without too many scientific words and phrases. We strongly believe in credible information and modern science.

Program

The “Program” page is where you can find our collection of puzzles. Once logged in, browse the diverse selections of puzzles we have including sudoku, riddles and analogies!

Technology

Lowering barriers of access through technology is our priority. Our online environment grants anyone with internet connection access to our programs and resources.

Science

Our methods have been studied extensively by researchers from credible research institutions around the world. No pseudoscience. No nonsense.

One way to reduce risk of dementia is to keep the mind active. The puzzles in our collection have been shown to help slow memory-loss and other cognitive issues.
